About us

The best site for detergents

Our challenge begins at the beginning of 2019. Like many Italians residing abroad, we shared the need to have Italian products at home at the right price and without too much effort. So we asked ourselves: is it possible to offer a reliable shipping service with suppliers coming only from the Italian market? We knew it was going to be a great challenge and that it would involve huge sacrifices.


Without the support of any external funds, our idea was realized only thanks to our savings. We preferred to quit our job in order to dedicate ourselves 100% to the life of AdiTrade.


We wanted at all costs to take care of all the phases of our work closely, from the choice of Italian products to the packaging and shipping of packages. In order to take care of your box with our hands, we have provided our own warehouseand we have been forced to divide ourselves between Italy and Amsterdam.


After a year of activity, our challenge continues! With a team made up of young and very young people, who support us both in Italy and from abroad, we study the development of AdiTrade step by step.

A service that increasingly satisfies the needs of our AdiTrade-lovers. Your joy is the motivation to achieve our goal: to ship Italy to your home!

Passion for Italy

Being at the table is the primary link of us Italians in the world. If in Italy we prefer only our regional products, abroad we appreciate any product as long as it is Italian.

Expertise in the sector

Our mission is to send Italy to the home of Italians residing in Europe and England! We are experts in the field of shipping abroad and we love Italian products.

Openness to the world

We look to the world from Italy! We know closely the difficulties that Italians have to face living abroad. We work for you and to make you feel closer to home.

Italian brands

We offer an e-commerce for the online shopping of products that come only from Italian suppliers. We want to fill the homes of Italians abroad with products that they would find when shopping in Italy.

Italian prices

With us it's like shopping at your trusted supermarket in Italy. We want to send a pinch of Italy to all residents abroad and support you in our small way.
